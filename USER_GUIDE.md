## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account
1. Visit the EatWise app
2. Click "Sign Up" on the login page
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a secure password
5. Check your email for verification link

### Setting Up Your Profile
After signing up, complete your health profile with:
- **Age Group**: 20-25, 26-35, 36-45, 46-55, 56+

- **Dietary Preferences**: Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.
- **Health Goals**: Weight loss, muscle gain, athletic performance, general wellness
- **Timezone**: For accurate daily resets
- **Daily Water Goal**: Default is 8 glasses (can adjust)

## Meal Logging

### Method 1: Text Description üìù

**Steps**:
1. Click "Log Meal" ‚Üí "Text Input"
2. Describe your meal naturally, e.g.:
   - "Grilled chicken breast with brown rice and steamed broccoli"
   - "Turkey sandwich with lettuce, tomato, and whole wheat bread"
   - "Greek salad with olive oil dressing"
3. Click "Analyze Meal"
4. Review the AI's analysis:
   - Detected foods
   - Nutritional breakdown
   - Healthiness score (0-100)
   - Health notes
5. Click "Save Meal"
6. **Reward**: +25 XP for meal logging

**Best Practices**:
- Be specific about portions ("1 chicken breast", "2 slices bread")
- Include cooking methods (grilled, fried, baked)
- Mention sauces and dressings
- Log as soon as you can remember details
- For packaged foods, include the brand name

### Method 2: Photo Recognition üì∏

**Steps**:
1. Click "Log Meal" ‚Üí "Photo Upload"
2. Take a clear, well-lit photo of your meal
3. Click "Choose Image" and select from your device
4. The AI will detect:
   - Individual food items
   - Estimated portions
   - Total nutrition
5. Review and adjust if needed
6. Click "Save Meal"
7. **Reward**: +25 XP for meal logging

**Photo Tips**:
- Good lighting is essential
- Place meal on contrasting background
- Take photos before eating
- Capture from above for best recognition
- Include all components (plate, sides, drinks)

### Meal Logging Streaks üî•

- **Current Streak**: Days in a row you've logged at least one meal
- **Longest Streak**: Your personal record
- **Motivation**: Each day extends your streak; missing one resets it
- **Milestones**: Get notified at 3-day, 7-day, 14-day, 30-day marks

## Restaurant Menu Analyzer

### Purpose
Helps you make healthy choices when eating out by analyzing restaurant menus.

### How It Works

**Step 1: Get the Menu**
- Option A: Upload a menu photo (takes a picture of the menu)
- Option B: Paste menu text (from restaurant website)

**Step 2: Upload/Paste**
1. Click "Eating Out" in sidebar
2. Choose upload method:
   - üì∏ Click to upload photo of menu
   - üìù Paste text from menu
3. Click "Analyze Menu"

**Step 3: Review Analysis**
The AI provides:
- ‚úÖ **Best Options for You**: Top recommendations based on your health profile
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Items to Avoid**: Dishes that don't align with your goals
- üí° **Cuisine Tips**: Restaurant-specific healthy eating advice
- ü•ó **Full Nutrition Cards**: Detailed breakdown for each analyzed item

### Example Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Italian Restaurant**
- Upload their menu
- AI identifies pasta dishes and their nutritional content
- Recommends whole wheat options
- Suggests grilled fish instead of cream-based dishes
- Provides tips: "Choose olive oil-based sauces"

**Scenario 2: Fast Casual**
- Paste their menu from website
- AI analyzes protein, calories, sodium
- Recommends customizations: "Get dressing on the side"
- Highlights lowest-calorie options

**Scenario 3: Asian Cuisine**
- Upload menu photo
- AI evaluates soy sauce (sodium), fried items
- Recommends steamed options
- Gives tips: "Ask for sauce on the side"

### Best Practices

1. **High-quality photos**: Well-lit, clear photos work best
2. **Complete menus**: Include appetizers, mains, sides
3. **Dietary context**: Tell coach about allergies/preferences
4. **Ask for details**: Request nutrition info before ordering

## FAQ

### Meal Logging

**Q: How accurate is the AI food recognition?**
A: Very accurate! It uses advanced AI trained on thousands of meals. It's typically 90%+ accurate, but always review and adjust if needed.

**Q: Can I edit a meal after logging?**
A: Currently, you can delete and re-log. Use the history feature to quickly find and relog similar meals.

**Q: What if the AI misidentifies my meal?**
A: Scroll down to see suggested corrections. If needed, describe the meal differently next time or use text input instead of photo.

### Gamification

**Q: Why didn't I get XP for logging a meal?**
A: You get +25 XP for every meal logged. If you don't see it, try:
- Refresh the page
- Reopen the app
- Check that meal was actually saved

**Q: Can I lose XP?**
A: No, XP only increases. You can't go backwards in levels.

**Q: What happens if I miss a daily challenge?**
A: Nothing! New challenges appear tomorrow. Challenges don't penalize; they only reward.

**Q: My streak reset‚Äîhow do I rebuild it?**
A: Log at least one meal per day. The new streak counter starts fresh. You can beat your longest streak!

### Coaching

**Q: Is the coach really AI or a real person?**
A: It's AI! But trained on nutrition science and personalized to you. Available 24/7!

**Q: Can the coach help with medical advice?**
A: The coach provides general nutrition guidance, not medical advice. For medical concerns, consult a doctor.

**Q: Does the coach remember my previous conversations?**
A: Only within the current session. It uses your profile and meal history for context.

### Water Tracking

**Q: How do I log water?**
A: Use the buttons on your dashboard:
- ‚ûï Add one glass
- ‚ûñ Remove a glass
- üèÅ Jump to goal

**Q: Can I customize my daily water goal?**
A: Yes! Go to My Profile ‚Üí Edit ‚Üí set your Daily Water Goal.

**Q: Why is hydration important?**
A: Water helps nutrition absorption, energy, focus, and overall health. The Hydration Hero challenge encourages consistent drinking.

### General

**Q: How often are my nutrition targets updated?**
A: Automatically based on your profile, age group, and health conditions. They adjust when you update your profile.

**Q: Can I share my progress with others?**
A: Currently, data is private. Sharing features planned for future updates.

**Q: What if I have an issue or bug?**
A: Contact support through the app or report on GitHub: https://github.com/scmlewis/eatwise_ai

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes. Go to My Profile and request data deletion. This is permanent.
