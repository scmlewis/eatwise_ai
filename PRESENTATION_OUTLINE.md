## Presentation Tips

### Delivery:
1. **Pacing:** 3-5 minutes per slide for 20-25 slides = ~20 minute presentation
2. **Storytelling:** Lead with the problem, present your solution, show the demo
3. **Engagement:** Ask questions, get feedback, encourage discussion
4. **Energy:** Be passionate about the problem you're solving

### Slide Design Tips:
- Use consistent branding (teal color scheme from your app)
- Keep text minimal (bullets, not paragraphs)
- Use high-quality images and screenshots
- Maintain visual hierarchy
- Include speaker notes for detailed talking points

### Practice:
- Rehearse multiple times
- Time yourself
- Prepare answers to likely questions
- Have backup slides for deep dives
- Record a version for sharing

### Visual Design Recommendations:
- **Color Palette:** Teal (#10A19D), Purple (#845EF7), Dark background
- **Font:** Outfit (matches your app)
- **Icons:** Use consistent icons throughout
- **Transitions:** Subtle, professional (avoid flashy animations)
